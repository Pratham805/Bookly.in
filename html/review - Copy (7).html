Become and Engineer not just an Engineering Graduate

By SHIVAKUMAR PALANIAPPAN

  • Equinox
  • Author: David Towsey
  • Publisher: Ad Astra
  • ISBN: 9781801101653
  • Published: May 2022
  • Pages: 336
  • Format reviewed: Hardback
  • Review date: 12/05/2022
  • Language: English

Review

1] This book rightly addressed the current situation of Engineering education. Though we have a very high number of engineering colleges in the southern part of the country, we still have the gap between engineering education and the industry requirements. We have millions of engineers graduating every year, but still the industry is not happy with the standard of knowledge which an engineer has. Though we are having the engineering degrees, but we lack the skill required for the industry. Even the government has taken up initiatives to close this GAP.
This particular book "Become an engineer not just an Engineering Graduate" is an excellent book, which focuses on closing this gap. The author has did a great research on engineering education and wrote this book addressing the students. The one who reads this book will know how to close the GAP and becomes industry ready. Really Great book.

2] I want to thank Mr.Sivakumar Palaniappan sir for this wonderful book. I would suggest this book to the students who want to do engineering, parents who want their children to get into engineering, engineering students and staffs of engineering college. The difference between Engineers and Engineering graduates has been portrayed.
Every aspect of engineering has been explained clearly. The best part is the difference between "HOBBIES AND TIME WASTERS". Being an Engineering student I realized where I stand after reading this book. I wish I had read this book four years ago. This book will definitely bring a change in engineer/engineering graduates' life. I will give 5 out of 5!

3] Engineering is a career by default for most of the people in our part of the world. With the increasing population of engineering students and decrease in projected engineering jobs, selection of engineering as a career has to be done with clarity.
Sivakumar Palaniappan's book comes to the rescue. A must read for engineering aspirants and people without any aspirations. This book is lucidly presented and presents with great clarity on why to choose engineering and more importantly why not to choose engineering.
The book is an easy read and Sivakumar Palaniappan adds a personal touch to each chapters with his anecdotes. The book is a relevant one for engineering students, past, present and future.

4] Dear Readers,
I have read throught this book completely, its worth reading as many times as possible. I would say that, this book is not only for engineering grads but for anyone who wants to be the architect of his own future!
The interesting part is, the author has linked every tale(unit) of the book with an live story which is so close to our own lives but we must have not encashed it though there is an lesson to learn. Special about this book: No difficult words, sentences, no unrealstic stories, useful to any age group, easily helps reader to connect to our own lives.
So my take away..pl buy a copy and useas a pocket calender whcih will help you at any point of time in life.worth investing in reading.

5] I am a Psychology student with catholic views, which brought me a interest in understand knowing how an ideal Engineering graduate should be like. I gained myriad of insights through it. A conventional adage goes this way, "By three methods we may learn wisdom: First, by reflection, which is noblest; Second, by imitation, which is easiest; and third by experience, which is the bitterest".
A lot of students choose Engineering as their career without even knowing what it is. I would appreciate if lot of students read this book before taking up Engineering as their career.
It's phenomenal!

Summary And Purpose

This book Become an Engineer Not just an Engineering Graduate is written with the aim of encouraging engineering graduates to be passionate engineers, help them enhance their employ-ability potential and enable them to sculpt their career as exuberant engineers. Need of the hour is not just the IT professionals but exuberant engineers who have the courage to confidently take-on the technological challenges of today and tomorrow. We need engineers and scientists who can understand the ground reality, with grass roots exposure, clarity of basic engineering concepts and ability to put all these together and think in a collective perspective to find technical solutions to all the haunting social problems. This book is written by a leading blogger and career coach with over a decade of experience who has taken his own career like a grand experiment, transitioning from core electronics engineering to human resources to blogging to public speaking to corporate training to career consulting and coaching. Every chapter starts with a real life student tale that brings in the right significance to the chapter and relevance to its contents. This book is not to read at once and keep it back in the book shelf. It should be your life guide and a reference for your long term career crafting process.

Written on 12th May 2022 by Sam Tyler .
